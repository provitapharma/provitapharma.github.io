PROVITA COMPETITION

Research and Business section

Hi Everyone!

Provita Pharmaceuticals is planning on hosting a new competition, the Provita Competition, for high-schoolers to enter and create an idea for a novel pharmaceutical product.
Participants can enter in one of two sectors: Research or Business. In the Research sector, participants (in groups of 1-3 people) will have to detail their product and the basic experiment process. In the Business sector, participants will have to give a 10 minute (max) pitch for their product to convince investors to invest in it. All entries will be judged by Provita Pharmaceutical Business and Research students.
The competition is free to participate in and will have prizes, which will valued up to $200!
If you are interested in participating, please fill out this form so we can gauge interest and best accommodate for all entries! This form is NOT BINDING. In the coming weeks, we will send out a sign up form, video detailing instructions, and further resources for the competition. If you have any questions, please contact the Provita at provitabca@gmail.com. Thank you and good luck!
