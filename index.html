PROVITA COMPETITION

Research and Business section

IDEA

There are two categories participants can enter in: Research and Business. In both cases, they need to come up with an idea for a novel pharmaceutical product. In the Research sector, they will detail what their product is and their basic experimental process. This includes a research question, hypothesis, materials, and a basic outline of the experimental procedures and data analysis. In Business they will give a 10 minute (max) pitch for their products, trying to convince investors to invest in it. This means they need a SWOT, 4 P’s, and a breakeven analysis. Scientific information does not have to be completely accurate but the product must be very plausible for business. They will present their work in a 10 minute video of their creation, which they will submit. Provita business and research kids will judge them.
